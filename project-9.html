This was my final project for Art 74.

For the final project in ART 74 I have chosen to create video game photography series as a form of new media art. The photo series is printed out and framed, allowing it to be seen hanging on a wall or placed on a table. The old effect makes the photos seem real and covers the video game aspect from first time viewers. The content itself is photography of the video game Red Dead Redemption 2, a western cowboy game set in 1899. The in-game camera feature is used to create realistic photographs relevant to its time. Photos range from different environments and self portrait of various social classes.
